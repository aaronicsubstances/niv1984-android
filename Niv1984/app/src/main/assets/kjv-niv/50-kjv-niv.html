  1. (NIV) so that through my being with you again your joy in Christ Jesus will overflow on account of me.

  1. (KJV) Only let your conversation be as it becometh the gospel of Christ: that whether I come and see you, or else be absent, I may hear of your affairs, that ye stand fast in one spirit, with one mind striving together for the faith of the gospel;

  1. (KJV) And in nothing terrified by your adversaries: which is to them an evident token of perdition, but to you of salvation, and that of God.
  1. (NIV) For it has been granted to you on behalf of Christ not only to believe on him, but also to suffer for him,
  1. (KJV) For unto you it is given in the behalf of Christ, not only to believe on him, but also to suffer for his sake;
  1. (NIV) since you are going through the same struggle you saw I had, and now hear that I still have.
