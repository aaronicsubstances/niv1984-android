Psalm 3

  1. Psalm 3 A psalm of David. When he fled from his son Absalom.
  2. O LORD, how many are my foes! How many rise up against me!
  3. Many are saying of me, "God will not deliver him." Selah [1]
  4. But you are a shield around me, O LORD; you bestow glory on me and lift [2] up my head.
  5. To the LORD I cry aloud, and he answers me from his holy hill. Selah
  6. I lie down and sleep; I wake again, because the LORD sustains me.
  7. I will not fear the tens of thousands drawn up against me on every side.
  8. Arise, O LORD! Deliver me, O my God! Strike all my enemies on the jaw; break the teeth of the wicked.
  9. From the LORD comes deliverance. May your blessing be on your people. Selah
  1. [2] A word of uncertain meaning, occurring frequently in the Psalms; possibly a musical term
  2. [3] Or LORD, my Glorious One, who lifts
