Psalm 11

  1. Psalm 11 For the director of music. Of David.
  2. In the LORD I take refuge. How then can you say to me: "Flee like a bird to your mountain.
  3. For look, the wicked bend their bows; they set their arrows against the strings to shoot from the shadows at the upright in heart.
  4. When the foundations are being destroyed, what can the righteous do [1] ?"
  5. The LORD is in his holy temple; the LORD is on his heavenly throne. He observes the sons of men; his eyes examine them.
  6. The LORD examines the righteous, but the wicked [2] and those who love violence his soul hates.
  7. On the wicked he will rain fiery coals and burning sulfur; a scorching wind will be their lot.
  8. For the LORD is righteous, he loves justice; upright men will see his face.
  1. [3] Or what is the Righteous One doing
  2. [5] Or The LORD, the Righteous One, examines the wicked,
