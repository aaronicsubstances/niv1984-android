Psalm 12

  1. Psalm 12 For the director of music. According to sheminith. [1] A psalm of David.
  2. Help, LORD, for the godly are no more; the faithful have vanished from among men.
  3. Everyone lies to his neighbor; their flattering lips speak with deception.
  4. May the LORD cut off all flattering lips and every boastful tongue
  5. that says, "We will triumph with our tongues; we own our lips [2] --who is our master?"
  6. "Because of the oppression of the weak and the groaning of the needy, I will now arise," says the LORD. "I will protect them from those who malign them."
  7. And the words of the LORD are flawless, like silver refined in a furnace of clay, purified seven times.
  8. O LORD, you will keep us safe and protect us from such people forever.
  9. The wicked freely strut about when what is vile is honored among men.
  1. [Title: Probably a musical term
  2. [4] Or our lips are our plowshares
