Psalm 15

  1. Psalm 15 A psalm of David.
  2. LORD, who may dwell in your sanctuary? Who may live on your holy hill?
  3. He whose walk is blameless and who does what is righteous, who speaks the truth from his heart
  4. and has no slander on his tongue, who does his neighbor no wrong and casts no slur on his fellowman,
  5. who despises a vile man but honors those who fear the LORD, who keeps his oath even when it hurts,
  6. who lends his money without usury and does not accept a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things will never be shaken.
