Psalm 16

  1. Psalm 16 A miktam [1] of David.
  2. Keep me safe, O God, for in you I take refuge.
  3. I said to the LORD, "You are my Lord; apart from you I have no good thing."
  4. As for the saints who are in the land, they are the glorious ones in whom is all my delight. [2]
  5. The sorrows of those will increase who run after other gods. I will not pour out their libations of blood or take up their names on my lips.
  6. LORD, you have assigned me my portion and my cup; you have made my lot secure.
  7. The boundary lines have fallen for me in pleasant places; surely I have a delightful inheritance.
  8. I will praise the LORD, who counsels me; even at night my heart instructs me.
  9. I have set the LORD always before me. Because he is at my right hand, I will not be shaken.
  10. Therefore my heart is glad and my tongue rejoices; my body also will rest secure,
  11. because you will not abandon me to the grave, [3] nor will you let your Holy One [4] see decay.
  12. You have made [5] known to me the path of life; you will fill me with joy in your presence, with eternal pleasures at your right hand.
