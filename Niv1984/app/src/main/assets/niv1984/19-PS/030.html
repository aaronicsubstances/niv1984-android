Psalm 30

  1. Psalm 30 A psalm. A song. For the dedication of the temple. [1] Of David.
  2. I will exalt you, O LORD, for you lifted me out of the depths and did not let my enemies gloat over me.
  3. O LORD my God, I called to you for help and you healed me.
  4. O LORD, you brought me up from the grave [2] ; you spared me from going down into the pit.
  5. Sing to the LORD, you saints of his; praise his holy name.
  6. For his anger lasts only a moment, but his favor lasts a lifetime; weeping may remain for a night, but rejoicing comes in the morning.
  7. When I felt secure, I said, "I will never be shaken."
  8. O LORD, when you favored me, you made my mountain [3] stand firm; but when you hid your face, I was dismayed.
  9. To you, O LORD, I called; to the Lord I cried for mercy:
  10. "What gain is there in my destruction, [4] in my going down into the pit? Will the dust praise you? Will it proclaim your faithfulness?
  11. Hear, O LORD, and be merciful to me; O LORD, be my help."
  12. You turned my wailing into dancing; you removed my sackcloth and clothed me with joy,
  13. that my heart may sing to you and not be silent. O LORD my God, I will give you thanks forever.
  1. [Title: Or palace
  2. [3] Hebrew Sheol
  3. [7] Or hill country
  4. [9] Or there if I am silenced
