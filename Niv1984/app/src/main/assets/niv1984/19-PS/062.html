Psalm 62

  1. Psalm 62 For the director of music. For Jeduthun. A psalm of David.
  2. My soul finds rest in God alone; my salvation comes from him.
  3. He alone is my rock and my salvation; he is my fortress, I will never be shaken.
  4. How long will you assault a man? Would all of you throw him down-- this leaning wall, this tottering fence?
  5. They fully intend to topple him from his lofty place; they take delight in lies. With their mouths they bless, but in their hearts they curse. Selah
  6. Find rest, O my soul, in God alone; my hope comes from him.
  7. He alone is my rock and my salvation; he is my fortress, I will not be shaken.
  8. My salvation and my honor depend on God [1] ; he is my mighty rock, my refuge.
  9. Trust in him at all times, O people; pour out your hearts to him, for God is our refuge. Selah
  10. Lowborn men are but a breath, the highborn are but a lie; if weighed on a balance, they are nothing; together they are only a breath.
  11. Do not trust in extortion or take pride in stolen goods; though your riches increase, do not set your heart on them.
  12. One thing God has spoken, two things have I heard: that you, O God, are strong,
  13. and that you, O Lord, are loving. Surely you will reward each person according to what he has done.
  1. [7] Or God Most High is my salvation and my honor
