Psalm 67

  1. Psalm 67 For the director of music. With stringed instruments. A psalm. A song.
  2. May God be gracious to us and bless us and make his face shine upon us, Selah
  3. that your ways may be known on earth, your salvation among all nations.
  4. May the peoples praise you, O God; may all the peoples praise you.
  5. May the nations be glad and sing for joy, for you rule the peoples justly and guide the nations of the earth. Selah
  6. May the peoples praise you, O God; may all the peoples praise you.
  7. Then the land will yield its harvest, and God, our God, will bless us.
  8. God will bless us, and all the ends of the earth will fear him.
