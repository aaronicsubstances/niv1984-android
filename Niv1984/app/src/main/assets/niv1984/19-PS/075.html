Psalm 75

  1. Psalm 75 For the director of music. [To the tune of] "Do Not Destroy." A psalm of Asaph. A song.
  2. We give thanks to you, O God, we give thanks, for your Name is near; men tell of your wonderful deeds.
  3. You say, "I choose the appointed time; it is I who judge uprightly.
  4. When the earth and all its people quake, it is I who hold its pillars firm. Selah
  5. To the arrogant I say, `Boast no more,' and to the wicked, `Do not lift up your horns.
  6. Do not lift your horns against heaven; do not speak with outstretched neck.'"
  7. No one from the east or the west or from the desert can exalt a man.
  8. But it is God who judges: He brings one down, he exalts another.
  9. In the hand of the LORD is a cup full of foaming wine mixed with spices; he pours it out, and all the wicked of the earth drink it down to its very dregs.
  10. As for me, I will declare this forever; I will sing praise to the God of Jacob.
  11. I will cut off the horns of all the wicked, but the horns of the righteous will be lifted up.
