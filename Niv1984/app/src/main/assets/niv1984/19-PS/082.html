Psalm 82

  1. Psalm 82 A psalm of Asaph.
  2. God presides in the great assembly; he gives judgment among the "gods":
  3. "How long will you [1] defend the unjust and show partiality to the wicked? Selah
  4. Defend the cause of the weak and fatherless; maintain the rights of the poor and oppressed.
  5. Rescue the weak and needy; deliver them from the hand of the wicked.
  6. "They know nothing, they understand nothing. They walk about in darkness; all the foundations of the earth are shaken.
  7. "I said, `You are "gods"; you are all sons of the Most High.'
  8. But you will die like mere men; you will fall like every other ruler."
  9. Rise up, O God, judge the earth, for all the nations are your inheritance.
  1. [2] The Hebrew is plural.
