Psalm 93

  1. Psalm 93
  2. The LORD reigns, he is robed in majesty; the LORD is robed in majesty and is armed with strength. The world is firmly established; it cannot be moved.
  3. Your throne was established long ago; you are from all eternity.
  4. The seas have lifted up, O LORD, the seas have lifted up their voice; the seas have lifted up their pounding waves.
  5. Mightier than the thunder of the great waters, mightier than the breakers of the sea-- the LORD on high is mighty.
  6. Your statutes stand firm; holiness adorns your house for endless days, O LORD.
