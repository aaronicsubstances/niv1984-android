Psalm 99

  1. Psalm 99
  2. The LORD reigns, let the nations tremble; he sits enthroned between the cherubim, let the earth shake.
  3. Great is the LORD in Zion; he is exalted over all the nations.
  4. Let them praise your great and awesome name-- he is holy.
  5. The King is mighty, he loves justice-- you have established equity; in Jacob you have done what is just and right.
  6. Exalt the LORD our God and worship at his footstool; he is holy.
  7. Moses and Aaron were among his priests, Samuel was among those who called on his name; they called on the LORD and he answered them.
  8. He spoke to them from the pillar of cloud; they kept his statutes and the decrees he gave them.
  9. O LORD our God, you answered them; you were to Israel [1] a forgiving God, though you punished their misdeeds. [2]
  10. Exalt the LORD our God and worship at his holy mountain, for the LORD our God is holy.
  1. [8] Hebrew them
  2. [8] Or an avenger of the wrongs done to them
