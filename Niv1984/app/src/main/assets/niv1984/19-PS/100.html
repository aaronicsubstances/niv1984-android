Psalm 100

  1. Psalm 100 A psalm. For giving thanks.
  2. Shout for joy to the LORD, all the earth.
  3. Worship the LORD with gladness; come before him with joyful songs.
  4. Know that the LORD is God. It is he who made us, and we are his [1] ; we are his people, the sheep of his pasture.
  5. Enter his gates with thanksgiving and his courts with praise; give thanks to him and praise his name.
  6. For the LORD is good and his love endures forever; his faithfulness continues through all generations.
  1. [3] Or and not we ourselves
