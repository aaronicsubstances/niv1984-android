Psalm 114

  1. Psalm 114
  2. When Israel came out of Egypt, the house of Jacob from a people of foreign tongue,
  3. Judah became God's sanctuary, Israel his dominion.
  4. The sea looked and fled, the Jordan turned back;
  5. the mountains skipped like rams, the hills like lambs.
  6. Why was it, O sea, that you fled, O Jordan, that you turned back,
  7. you mountains, that you skipped like rams, you hills, like lambs?
  8. Tremble, O earth, at the presence of the Lord, at the presence of the God of Jacob,
  9. who turned the rock into a pool, the hard rock into springs of water.
