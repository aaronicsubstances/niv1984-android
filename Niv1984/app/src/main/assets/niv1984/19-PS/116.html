Psalm 116

  1. Psalm 116
  2. I love the LORD, for he heard my voice; he heard my cry for mercy.
  3. Because he turned his ear to me, I will call on him as long as I live.
  4. The cords of death entangled me, the anguish of the grave [1] came upon me; I was overcome by trouble and sorrow.
  5. Then I called on the name of the LORD: "O LORD, save me!"
  6. The LORD is gracious and righteous; our God is full of compassion.
  7. The LORD protects the simplehearted; when I was in great need, he saved me.
  8. Be at rest once more, O my soul, for the LORD has been good to you.
  9. For you, O LORD, have delivered my soul from death, my eyes from tears, my feet from stumbling,
  10. that I may walk before the LORD in the land of the living.
  11. I believed; therefore [2] I said, "I am greatly afflicted."
  12. And in my dismay I said, "All men are liars."
  13. How can I repay the LORD for all his goodness to me?
  14. I will lift up the cup of salvation and call on the name of the LORD.
  15. I will fulfill my vows to the LORD in the presence of all his people.
  16. Precious in the sight of the LORD is the death of his saints.
  17. O LORD, truly I am your servant; I am your servant, the son of your maidservant [3] ; you have freed me from my chains.
  18. I will sacrifice a thank offering to you and call on the name of the LORD.
  19. I will fulfill my vows to the LORD in the presence of all his people,
  20. in the courts of the house of the LORD-- in your midst, O Jerusalem. Praise the LORD. [4]
  1. [3] Hebrew Sheol
  2. [10] Or believed even when
  3. [16] Or servant, your faithful son
  4. [19] Hebrew Hallelu Yah
