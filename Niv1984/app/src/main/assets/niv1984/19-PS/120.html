Psalm 120

  1. Psalm 120 A song of ascents.
  2. I call on the LORD in my distress, and he answers me.
  3. Save me, O LORD, from lying lips and from deceitful tongues.
  4. What will he do to you, and what more besides, O deceitful tongue?
  5. He will punish you with a warrior's sharp arrows, with burning coals of the broom tree.
  6. Woe to me that I dwell in Meshech, that I live among the tents of Kedar!
  7. Too long have I lived among those who hate peace.
  8. I am a man of peace; but when I speak, they are for war.
