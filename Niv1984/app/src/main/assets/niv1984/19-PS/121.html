Psalm 121

  1. Psalm 121 A song of ascents.
  2. I lift up my eyes to the hills-- where does my help come from?
  3. My help comes from the LORD, the Maker of heaven and earth.
  4. He will not let your foot slip-- he who watches over you will not slumber;
  5. indeed, he who watches over Israel will neither slumber nor sleep.
  6. The LORD watches over you-- the LORD is your shade at your right hand;
  7. the sun will not harm you by day, nor the moon by night.
  8. The LORD will keep you from all harm-- he will watch over your life;
  9. the LORD will watch over your coming and going both now and forevermore.
