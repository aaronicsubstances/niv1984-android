Psalm 125

  1. Psalm 125 A song of ascents.
  2. Those who trust in the LORD are like Mount Zion, which cannot be shaken but endures forever.
  3. As the mountains surround Jerusalem, so the LORD surrounds his people both now and forevermore.
  4. The scepter of the wicked will not remain over the land allotted to the righteous, for then the righteous might use their hands to do evil.
  5. Do good, O LORD, to those who are good, to those who are upright in heart.
  6. But those who turn to crooked ways the LORD will banish with the evildoers. Peace be upon Israel.
