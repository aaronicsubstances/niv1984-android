Psalm 128

  1. Psalm 128 A song of ascents.
  2. Blessed are all who fear the LORD, who walk in his ways.
  3. You will eat the fruit of your labor; blessings and prosperity will be yours.
  4. Your wife will be like a fruitful vine within your house; your sons will be like olive shoots around your table.
  5. Thus is the man blessed who fears the LORD.
  6. May the LORD bless you from Zion all the days of your life; may you see the prosperity of Jerusalem,
  7. and may you live to see your children's children. Peace be upon Israel.
