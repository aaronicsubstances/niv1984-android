Psalm 130

  1. Psalm 130 A song of ascents.
  2. Out of the depths I cry to you, O LORD;
  3. O Lord, hear my voice. Let your ears be attentive to my cry for mercy.
  4. If you, O LORD, kept a record of sins, O Lord, who could stand?
  5. But with you there is forgiveness; therefore you are feared.
  6. I wait for the LORD, my soul waits, and in his word I put my hope.
  7. My soul waits for the Lord more than watchmen wait for the morning, more than watchmen wait for the morning.
  8. O Israel, put your hope in the LORD, for with the LORD is unfailing love and with him is full redemption.
  9. He himself will redeem Israel from all their sins.
