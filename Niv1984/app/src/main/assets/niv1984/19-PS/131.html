Psalm 131

  1. Psalm 131 A song of ascents. Of David.
  2. My heart is not proud, O LORD, my eyes are not haughty; I do not concern myself with great matters or things too wonderful for me.
  3. But I have stilled and quieted my soul; like a weaned child with its mother, like a weaned child is my soul within me.
  4. O Israel, put your hope in the LORD both now and forevermore.
