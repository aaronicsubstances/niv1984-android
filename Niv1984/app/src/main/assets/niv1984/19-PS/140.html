Psalm 140

  1. Psalm 140 For the director of music. A psalm of David.
  2. Rescue me, O LORD, from evil men; protect me from men of violence,
  3. who devise evil plans in their hearts and stir up war every day.
  4. They make their tongues as sharp as a serpent's; the poison of vipers is on their lips. Selah
  5. Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked; protect me from men of violence who plan to trip my feet.
  6. Proud men have hidden a snare for me; they have spread out the cords of their net and have set traps for me along my path. Selah
  7. O LORD, I say to you, "You are my God." Hear, O LORD, my cry for mercy.
  8. O Sovereign LORD, my strong deliverer, who shields my head in the day of battle--
  9. do not grant the wicked their desires, O LORD; do not let their plans succeed, or they will become proud. Selah
  10. Let the heads of those who surround me be covered with the trouble their lips have caused.
  11. Let burning coals fall upon them; may they be thrown into the fire, into miry pits, never to rise.
  12. Let slanderers not be established in the land; may disaster hunt down men of violence.
  13. I know that the LORD secures justice for the poor and upholds the cause of the needy.
  14. Surely the righteous will praise your name and the upright will live before you.
