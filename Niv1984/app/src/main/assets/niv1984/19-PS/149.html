Psalm 149

  1. Psalm 149
  2. Praise the LORD. [1] Sing to the LORD a new song, his praise in the assembly of the saints.
  3. Let Israel rejoice in their Maker; let the people of Zion be glad in their King.
  4. Let them praise his name with dancing and make music to him with tambourine and harp.
  5. For the LORD takes delight in his people; he crowns the humble with salvation.
  6. Let the saints rejoice in this honor and sing for joy on their beds.
  7. May the praise of God be in their mouths and a double-edged sword in their hands,
  8. to inflict vengeance on the nations and punishment on the peoples,
  9. to bind their kings with fetters, their nobles with shackles of iron,
  10. to carry out the sentence written against them. This is the glory of all his saints. Praise the LORD.
  1. [1] Hebrew Hallelu Yah; also in verse 9
