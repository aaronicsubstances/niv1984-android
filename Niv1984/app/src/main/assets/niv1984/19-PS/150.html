Psalm 150

  1. Psalm 150
  2. Praise the LORD. [1] Praise God in his sanctuary; praise him in his mighty heavens.
  3. Praise him for his acts of power; praise him for his surpassing greatness.
  4. Praise him with the sounding of the trumpet, praise him with the harp and lyre,
  5. praise him with tambourine and dancing, praise him with the strings and flute,
  6. praise him with the clash of cymbals, praise him with resounding cymbals.
  7. Let everything that has breath praise the LORD. Praise the LORD.
  1. [1] Hebrew Hallelu Yah; also in verse 6
